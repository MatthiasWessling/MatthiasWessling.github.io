## Thesis

- Title: Wettability and reaction visualization of electrochemical CO₂ reduction at gas diffusion electrodes
- Graduate Date: 2026-02-03

### Condensed Summary

- Porous media plays a significant role in processes like catalysis, membrane filtration, surface coatings or enhanced oil recovery.
- Particularly in electrochemical CO2 reduction (ecCO2RR), it is still unclear where exactly in the catalyst layer (CL) the reaction takes place.
- Abstract model porous media is used to understand wettability on the pore-scale and a realistic model electrolyzer with a silver-based GDE is developed to understand...
- The measurements showed, that regardless of the driving force for electrolyte imbibition, in all cases a similar steady state is reached.
- Thus, the wettability of the CL has a direct influence on the efficiency and selectivity of the process.
